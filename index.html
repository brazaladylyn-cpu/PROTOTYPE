Introduction

In today's digital world, protecting personal information has become more important than ever. With the rise of social media, online platforms, and digital transactions, people often share sensitive data without realizing the risks. Our digital prototype focuses on improving cybersecurity by helping users become more aware and cautious about the information they share online.

Materials Used

Created using OneCompiler for code development and real-time previewing.

Problem

Many users unknowingly expose personal information such as their location, contact details, and private data on the Internet. This increases the risk of identity theft, scams, and hacking. The lack of awareness and control over shared data makes users vulnerable to cyber threats.

Solution

To address the issue, we created an app that limits and manages the personal information users share online. The app guides users in making safer decisions by monitoring, filtering, and controlling the type of information they disclose on different platforms.

Main Function

The app's main function is to analyze and limit the sharing of personal information. It alerts users when they are about to share sensitive data, provides recommendations for safer alternatives, and helps manage privacy settings across platforms.

User Benefits

1. Protects personal and sensitive information.
2. Reduces the risk of cyber attacks and identity theft.
3. Increases awareness of online privacy and security.
4. Provides better control over digital presence.

Conclusion

Overall, CyberGuard aims to create a safer online environment by empowering users to manage their personal information responsibly. By increasing awareness and providing practical tools, the app helps reduce cyber risk and promotes better cybersecurity habits in everyday digital life.
